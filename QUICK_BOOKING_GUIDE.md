# Quick Booking Guide: How to Book Your Transfer

## 🚗 Simple 5-Step Process

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  STEP 1: Fill Out Form                                   │
│  • Contact info (name, email, phone)                     │
│  • Transfer details (date, time, locations)               │
│  • Flight number (optional but helpful!)                  │
│  • Special requests                                       │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
                    ↓
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  STEP 2: Submit Request                                  │
│  • Click "Book Transfer"                                  │
│  • Receive immediate acknowledgment                      │
│  • Your request is sent to our team                      │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
                    ↓
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  STEP 3: Team Reviews                                    │
│  • We check availability (1-2 hours)                      │
│  • Confirm all details                                    │
│  • Prepare for your transfer                             │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
                    ↓
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  STEP 4: Payment Discussion                              │
│  • We contact you to discuss payment                      │
│  • Choose your method (CashApp, PayPal, etc.)            │

└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
                    ↓
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  STEP 5: Confirmation & Transfer                         │

│  • Enjoy your premium transfer!                          │

```

## ❓ Quick FAQ

**Q: How do I know my booking is confirmed?**  
A: You'll receive a confirmation with booking number (e.g., KT-2024-001234)

**Q: Can I change my booking?**  
A: Yes! Contact us with your booking number and requested changes

**Q: What if my flight is delayed?**  
A: If you provided flight number, we track it automatically. Otherwise, just contact us!

**Q: When do I pay?**  
A: We'll discuss payment timing and method after booking approval

**Q: Can I cancel?**  
A: Yes, contact us as soon as possible. We'll discuss refund options
