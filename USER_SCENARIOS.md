## Scenario 1: First-Time Booking via Web Form

### Step-by-Step Process

**Step 1: Fill Out the Booking Form**

- Go to the Transfer Service page on our website
- Fill in your contact information:
  - Full name
  - Email address
  - Phone number
- Enter transfer details:
  - Date and time of transfer
  - Pickup location
  - Drop-off address
  - Number of passengers
  - Number of luggage pieces
- Add any special notes or requests

**Step 2: Submit Your Request**

- Click "Book Transfer" button
- You'll receive an immediate confirmation message
- Your request is sent to our team

**Step 3: Receive Acknowledgment**

- Within minutes, you'll receive an acknowledgment via:
  - Email (if you provided one)
  - WhatsApp (if you prefer)
- The message confirms we received your request

**Step 4: Wait for Approval**

- Our team reviews your request
- We check availability and confirm details
- This usually takes 1-2 hours during business hours

**Step 5: Receive Confirmation**

- Once approved, you'll receive a confirmation with:
  - Your booking number (e.g., KT-2024-001234)
  - All transfer details
  - Contact information for questions
  - Payment information

**Step 6: Payment Discussion**

- Our team will contact you to discuss payment
- You can pay via your preferred method:
  - CashApp
  - PayPal
  - Venmo
  - Bank Transfer
  - Wise/Revolut
  - Cash (on arrival)
- We'll confirm once payment is received

**Step 7: Transfer Day**

- On the day of your transfer:
  - Our driver will arrive at the scheduled time
  - You'll receive real-time updates if needed
  - Enjoy your premium transfer service!

## Scenario 2: Booking with Flight Information

### When You Have a Flight Number

**Step 1: Enter Your Flight Number**

- In the booking form, enter your flight number (e.g., AF123, LH456)
- The system automatically searches for your flight information
- If found, you'll see:
  - Airline name
  - Departure and arrival airports
  - Scheduled arrival time
  - Terminal and gate (if available)

**Step 2: Use Flight Information**

- Click "Use Flight Info" to auto-fill:
  - Transfer date (from flight arrival date)
  - Transfer time (from flight arrival time)
  - Terminal and gate information
- You can still modify these if needed

**Step 3: Complete the Form**

- Add pickup location (airport terminal)
- Add drop-off address
- Specify number of passengers and luggage
- Add any special notes

**Step 4: Submit and Confirm**

- Submit your booking
- Our system tracks your flight status
- If your flight is delayed, we'll adjust pickup time automatically
- You'll receive updates about any changes

**Benefits:**

- ✅ Automatic date/time from flight
- ✅ Flight tracking for delays
- ✅ Terminal and gate information
- ✅ Less manual entry

## FAQ: Common Questions

### Booking Process

**Q: How far in advance should I book?**
A: We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance for best availability. However, we accept last-minute requests and will do our best to accommodate you.

**Q: Can I modify my booking after submission?**
A: Yes! Contact us via WhatsApp, email, or phone with your booking number, and we'll update your booking. Changes are subject to availability.

**Q: What if I need to cancel?**
A: Contact us as soon as possible. Cancellation policies depend on timing and circumstances. We'll discuss refund options based on your situation.

**Q: How do I know my booking is confirmed?**
A: You'll receive a confirmation email/WhatsApp message with your booking number (e.g., KT-2024-001234). Keep this number for reference.

### Payment

**Q: When do I need to pay?**
A: Payment timing is flexible. We'll discuss payment options after booking approval. You can pay in advance or on arrival (arranged in advance).

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept CashApp, PayPal, Venmo, Bank Transfer, Wise, Revolut, and Cash (when arranged). We'll discuss the best option for you.

**Q: Is payment secure?**
A: Yes! We use established third-party payment platforms (CashApp, PayPal, etc.) that handle security. We never store your payment information.

**Q: What if I have payment issues?**
A: Contact us immediately. We'll work with you to resolve any payment problems and find an alternative solution.

### Flight Information

**Q: Do I need to provide flight information?**
A: It's optional but recommended. If you provide your flight number, we can:

- Auto-fill transfer date and time
- Track flight delays
- Know your terminal and gate
- Adjust pickup time automatically

**Q: What if my flight is delayed?**
A: If you provided your flight number, we track delays automatically. We'll adjust your pickup time accordingly and notify you. If you didn't provide flight info, contact us with the new arrival time.

**Q: What if I can't find my flight in the system?**
A: No problem! You can manually enter:

- Arrival airport
- Terminal
- Gate
- Scheduled arrival time

### Communication

**Q: How will you contact me?**
A: We'll use your preferred method:

- Email for detailed confirmations
- WhatsApp for quick updates
- Phone for urgent matters
- You can specify your preference in the booking form

**Q: Can I contact you before my transfer?**
A: Absolutely! Use WhatsApp for quick questions, email for detailed requests, or call us for urgent matters. We're here to help.

**Q: Will I get updates on transfer day?**
A: Yes! You'll receive:

- Driver assignment notification
- Driver contact information
- Real-time updates if requested
- Arrival confirmation

### Special Requests

**Q: Can I request a specific driver?**
A: Yes, if you've used a driver before and liked their service, mention it in your booking notes. We'll try to assign the same driver when possible.

**Q: What if I have accessibility needs?**
A: Please mention this in your booking notes or contact us directly. We'll ensure appropriate vehicle and assistance are provided.

**Q: Can I make multiple stops?**
A: Yes! Mention this in your booking notes or contact us. Additional stops may affect pricing, which we'll discuss with you.

### After Transfer

**Q: How do I book again?**
A: Simply use the booking form again. If you've booked before, we'll recognize you and may suggest your previous preferences.

**Q: Can I leave feedback?**
A: Yes! After your transfer, you can:

- Send us a message via WhatsApp or email
- Leave a review (if requested)
- Contact us with any feedback

**Q: Do you offer loyalty programs?**
A: We value our returning clients! We track your booking history and may offer special rates or priority booking for frequent clients.
